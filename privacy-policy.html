Privacy Policy

A legal disclaimer

At CPTFIT, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in line with Australian privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Identity Details: Name, date of birth, gender
  • Contact Details: Email address, phone number, postal address
  • Health & Fitness Info: Medical history, fitness goals, injuries, dietary preferences
  • Payment Info: Transaction details (note: we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Website Usage Data: Interactions with our website or digital platforms

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide personal training, nutrition, and lifestyle coaching services
  • Manage bookings and communicate with you
  • Process payments securely
  • Improve our services and your experience
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Security

We implement strict security measures to protect your data, including:

  • Password-protected systems
  • Secure data storage
  • Access limited to authorised staff only

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your data only:

  • With trusted third-party providers (e.g. payment processors, booking platforms)
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, safety, or property of CPTFIT or others

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates
  • Request deletion (subject to legal and contractual obligations)
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications
