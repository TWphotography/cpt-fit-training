About

The Full Story

CPT FIT was born from my passion for fitness and a deep commitment to helping others unlock their full physical potential. With nationally recognised qualifications (Certificate III & IV in Fitness), I'm dedicated to coaching individuals of all fitness levels—guiding them to train smarter, move better, and achieve lasting results safely and effectively.

My journey began not in a gym, but on the racetrack. As a former open-wheel motorsport competitor at a high level—both in Australia and overseas—I've experienced firsthand how crucial both physical and mental conditioning are to peak performance. Whether you're chasing success in sport, work, or personal goals, fitness is the foundation.

Over the past three years, I've worked hands-on with a wide range of clients, helping them achieve real, measurable results—from weight loss and strength gains to improved mobility and confidence. My coaching is built on personal experience, practical knowledge, and a results-driven approach tailored to each individual.

I've had the privilege of learning from some of the best, including training with the CAMS Elite Driver Academy, Ben Cook's Fitness 2 Podium program, and completing my certifications through Fit College.

Now, I'm here to share that experience with you.

If you're ready to take the first step—or the next step—on your fitness journey, CPT FIT is here to help you train with purpose, confidence, and results.
