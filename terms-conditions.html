4. Qualifications and Scope of Practice

Our trainers are qualified under the following certifications:

  • Certificate III in Fitness (SIS30321): Enables trainers to work as gym instructors and group fitness instructors, providing fitness assessments, program development, and instruction in controlled environments such as fitness facilities.
  • Certificate IV in Fitness (SIS40221): Qualifies trainers to work as personal trainers, providing individually tailored client assessments, program development, instruction, and demonstration in various settings, including fitness facilities, outdoor locations, client homes, community spaces, and workplaces.

As registered professionals with AUSactive, we adhere to the Scope of Practice, ensuring services are delivered within the boundaries of our qualifications and competencies.
